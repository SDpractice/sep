06

Week starting 05 August 2019Partners interviews via Skype

Focus

Refine the initial findings from the initial workshop

  • - services content management (partner portal)
  • - enquiry handling

Refined needs

They all want minimal disruption from their actual ways of working, so:

  • - enquiries direct in their CRM
  • - events in their event provider to be fetch via API and added to SEP automatically
  • - ideally no duplication of product content sources
  • - knowledge base was not really something they wanted to use, and were happy not to have access to it. They would just use the SEP website as their knowledge base

30 Oct 2019F2F Research at Event

SEP Customer UAT

Focus
  • - Customer UAT - End to End testing
  • - Feedback email content, testing

Observations
  • - Issue around not opening a new tab when clicking on a Service/Product. It currently DOES open a new tab for Events
  • - Issue around not being obvious when a link is jumping off to another website

  • - Email text is too stuffy. It is what could be expected from Government, but represents the kind of email that people routinely delete on site.
